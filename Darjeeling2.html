Lepchajagad pine forests

Lepchajagat is a serene hamlet located at an altitude of 6959ft, concealed amidst the lush forest of rhododendron and oak trees. It is situated 19km from Darjeeling and is a hidden gem that is free from the tourist rush. The name Lepchajagat comes from the word "Jagat" which means "the world", so it was the world of the Lepchas. It was once used to be a hamlet of the Lepcha tribes³. Subsequently, it was taken over by the West Bengal Forest Development Corporation (WBFDC) and now a reserved forest area³. Lepchajagat with its serene ambiance and pictorial view of Mt. Kanchenjunga has bewitched the mind of many tourists traveling in Darjeeling³. Enclosed by the rich forested area, far from the maddening crowd and hassle of daily life, Lepchajagat has become the safe haven for tourists who are looking for solitude amidst wildlife³. The only sound which one can hear while visiting or staying in Lepchajagat is during day time the melodious chirping of exquisite Himalayan bird and at evening sound made by the small insects like cricket³. Witnessing the sunrise over the third highest mountain peak Mt. Kanchenjunga from the top of Lepchajagat can be a phenomenal experience for any nature lover.

Simana view point

Simana View Point in Darjeeling offers breathtaking vistas of the surrounding hills and valleys, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking panoramic views of the Himalayan landscape. This viewpoint provides a serene atmosphere, allowing visitors to relish the natural beauty of the region.

Nepal border or pashupti market

Pashupati Market in Darjeeling is a vibrant commercial hub situated near the Nepal border. This bustling market is known for its diverse range of goods, including clothing, accessories, electronics, and handicrafts. It serves as a focal point for both locals and tourists, offering a unique blend of Indian and Nepalese culture. The market's strategic location, close to the Nepal border, facilitates trade and cultural exchanges between the two nations, contributing to the dynamic atmosphere of this bustling marketplace.

Gopal Dhara tea garden

Gopal Dhara Tea Garden, situated in the enchanting hills of Darjeeling, is a revered name in the world of tea. With a history dating back to colonial times, this tea estate has earned its place among the elite in the Darjeeling tea industry. Covering acres of pristine land, Gopal Dhara boasts some of the most sought-after tea plantations in the region. Nestled amidst the Himalayan landscape, the garden's altitude and climate contribute to the unique flavor and aroma of its tea leaves. The tea from Gopal Dhara is celebrated for its delicate muscatel notes, floral undertones, and unparalleled quality. Each pluck is a testament to the dedication and expertise of the tea workers. Visitors to this tea estate are treated to breathtaking vistas of lush green tea bushes against the backdrop of the majestic Himalayas. The Gopal Dhara Tea Garden remains a testament to the enduring legacy of Darjeeling's tea tradition, and its teas continue to captivate connoisseurs around the globe.
